Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How do you determine project pricing? +
Our pricing is based on project complexity, timeline, and required expertise. We provide transparent, fixed-price quotes after our discovery call, with no hidden fees. Pricing includes all development, testing, documentation, and initial support period. We also offer flexible payment plans and can work within your budget constraints.
What if we need changes mid-project? +
We use an agile methodology that accommodates changes. Minor adjustments within scope are handled in our regular sprint cycles at no extra cost. For significant scope changes, we provide a change request assessment with time and cost implications, ensuring you maintain control over your project and budget.
Do you offer emergency support? +
Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency support for critical issues. All projects include a 30-day support period, and we offer ongoing maintenance packages with guaranteed response times. Emergency support ensures your business operations continue smoothly even when unexpected issues arise.
Can you work with our existing team? +
Absolutely! We excel at team augmentation and collaborative development. We can integrate seamlessly with your existing team, adapting to your workflows, tools, and communication preferences. We also provide knowledge transfer and documentation to ensure your team can maintain and extend the solutions we build.
What happens after project completion? +
Every project includes a 30-day support period with a scheduled check-in. We provide comprehensive documentation, training materials, and knowledge transfer. Many clients choose our ongoing support packages for continued optimization, feature additions, and technical support. We're committed to your long-term success.
